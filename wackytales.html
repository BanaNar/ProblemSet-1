The Box

The doorbell rang. Tony and Charlotte raced to the door. There on the doorstep was an enormous, beautiful box. What could be inside? They quickly took the box into the bedroom. Charlotte slowly put her eyes close to the box. She thought she heard a voice whisper, Hello!
